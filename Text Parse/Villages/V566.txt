V566 Lingqian 岭前 1. Village settlement Lingqian village is a part of the Songdong Administrative Village 松东行政村. There are approximately 500 people living in Lingqian village. 2. Surname groups The main surname groups are the Chen 陈 and the Zheng 郑. 3. Village temples 1) Yongxinggong 永兴宫 is co-owned with Pudun 埔墩 village. The main gods are Ciji zhenjun 慈济真君 and Taiyi xiangu 太乙仙姑. Secondary gods include Qitian dasheng 齐天大圣 and Puji shenghou 普济圣侯. 2) Yongxingshê 永兴社: built in 2003. The main gods are Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. 4. Rituals: Yuanxiao processions On 1/12 Zhanggong shengjun 张公圣君 of Fushougong 福寿宫 is invited to xingdao (practice the Dao) 行道 in every household. On 1/13 all the gods of Yongxinggong 永兴宫 are invited to go on procession within the village. On 1/17, the villagers take part in a procession of the gods through the thirteen jia 十三甲. Birthday celebration of the gods 1/15: Birthday celebration of Taiyi xiangu 太乙仙姑. 4/15: Birthday celebration of Ciji zhenjun 慈济真君.

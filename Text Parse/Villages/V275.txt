V275 Xuke 徐柯 1. Village settlement Xuke village includes Xuwei 徐尾 and Tiancuo 田厝. There are more than 500 people living in the village, 2. Surname groups Over 100 of villagers belong to the Ke 柯 surname group. 3. Village temples 1) Zhaoling shushê 昭灵书社 (which belongs to Xuke 徐柯) is dedicated to Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. 2) Zhaoling shengshê 昭灵圣社 (which also belongs to Xuwei 徐尾 and Tiancuo 田厝) is dedicated to Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. 4. Rituals: Yuanxiao processions On 1/15–16, Xuke 徐柯 takes the incense burners of the shê altar on a procession through their area. On 1/18, Yuanxiao 元宵 is held for Xuwei 徐尾 and Tiancuo 田厝, and the incense burners of the shê altar are taken on a procession through these areas. Birthday celebrations of the gods In the 10th lunar month, on a day determined by divination, a celebration is held for all the gods of the shê temple. Opera is performed for two days.

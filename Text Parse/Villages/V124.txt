V124 Kengbing 坑柄 1. Village settlement The village of Kengbing has approximately 500 people. 2. Surname groups The main surname groups are the Zhu 朱 and the Huang 黄. 3. Village temples 1) Xingheshê 兴和社 is dedicated to Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王, Houtu furen 后土夫人. 2) Yangxiandong 阳仙洞 is dedicated to Zhuo zhenren 卓真人. Left: Sun tongzi 孙童子. Right: Hu hufa 胡护法. 3) 狮山祠 Shishanci is dedicated to Lin Lonjiang 林龙江 (SJCDCB, 1992:37). 4. Rituals Yuanxiao processions On 1/19, Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人 are invited to go on procession within the village territory. Daoist masters are invited to conduct rites. Birthday celebrations of the gods 3/3: The birthday celebration of Xuantian shangdi 玄天上帝. Villagers participate in the procession of Beichengong 北辰宫 and Guchenggong 谷城宫. 6/12: The birthday celebration of Zhuo zhenren 卓真人. Marionette opera is performed. 8/13: The birthday celebration of Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. Opera is performed. Daoist masters are invited to conduct rites. 8/13–20: Yanggong taishi 杨公太师 of Guchenggong 谷城宫 is invited to watch opera. 11/15: The Xiayuan pudu 下元普度 rites of Universal Deliverance of the Lower Prime are conducted. Marionette opera is performed.

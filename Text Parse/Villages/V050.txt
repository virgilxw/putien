V050 Puwei 浦尾 1. Village settlement Puwei is part of the Baicheng Administrative Village 白埕行政村. Puwei village currently has more than 300 people. 2. Surname groups The main surname group is the Chen 陈. 3. Village temples 1) Yanqingshê 延庆社 is dedicated to Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. 2) Donglaigong 东来宫 is dedicated to Jialuo dasheng 伽罗大圣 and Tianshang shengmu 天上圣母. 4. Rituals: Yuanxiao processions On 1/9 the villagers take the incense burners of the shê altar to go to each household to xingdao 行道 practice the Dao. Birthday celebrations of the gods 3/10: The birthday celebration of Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. 3/23: The birthday celebration of Tianhou shengmu 天后圣母. Marionette plays are performed. 7/23: The birthday celebration of Zhanggong shengjun 张公圣君. The god is invited from the Dejitang 德济堂 to watch opera in the village. 8/19: The birthday celebration of Jialuo dasheng 伽罗大圣.

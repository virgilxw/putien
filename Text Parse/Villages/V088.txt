V088 Qiantang 钱塘 1. Village settlement Qiantang is part of Qijing Administrative Village 七境行政村. The village of Qiantang has a population of approximately 2000 people. 2. Surname groups The main surname group is the Peng 彭. Other surnames include the Zheng 郑, Liu 刘, Wu 吴, Chen 陈, and Huang 黄. The Peng are related to those living in Gangli 港利.

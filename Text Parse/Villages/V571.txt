V571 Lianhecun 联合村 1. Village settlement Lianhe (united village) includes Niushan 牛山, Caopu 草埔, Gongbian 宫边, and Jiucuofang 旧厝房. There are approximately 900 people living in these settlements. The main livelihoods are agriculture and fruit tree cultivation 2. Surname groups The main surname group is the Zhuang 庄. Other surnames include the Zhang 张 and the Xie 谢.

V527 Nanshan 南山 1. Village settlement Nanshan is divided into two neighborhoods: Shangnan 上南 and Zhongnan 中南. The total population is approximately 300 people. 2. Surname groups Surnames include the Zeng 曾, the Li 李, the Lin 林, the Chen 陈, the Shi 施, and the Zhang 张. There is no main surname group.

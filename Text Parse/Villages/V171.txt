V171 Dongxiang 东乡 1. Village settlement Dongxiang was originally known as Dongzhang 东张, and is part of the Dongfeng Administrative Village 东风行政. Dongxiang has a total population of approximately 2000 people. 2. Surname groups The main surname groups are the Chen 陈, the Zou 邹, the Weng 翁, and the Lin 林. The Chen make up two thirds of the population. 3. Village temples 1) Dongshanjing shiwushê 东山境石屋社 Canglonggong 苍龙宫. The main gods worshipped in the temple are Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王, Houtu furen 后土夫人, and Yanggong taishi 杨公太师.

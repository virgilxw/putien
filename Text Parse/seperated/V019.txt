V019 Xiacuo 下厝1. Village settlement Xiacuo village is part of the Zhengban AdministrativeVillage 郑阪行政村. The village has over 600 people.2. Surname groups The main surname group is the Wu 吴. They arerelated to the Wu of Dingcuo 顶厝.3. Village temples 1) Yongxing beishê 永兴北社 is co-owned by Dingcuo顶厝 and Xiacu 下厝. The main gods worshipped are Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. Secondary gods: Qianshi shengfei 钱氏圣妃,Dongyue zhusheng dadi 东岳注生大帝, and Wushi xianfei 吴氏仙妃.4. Rituals: Yuanxiao processions On 1/11, villagers from Xiacuo 下厝together with villagers from Dingcuo 顶厝 take Tiangong yuanshuai 田公元帅 and Lifu zhongjun 李府中军 on a procession around the two villagesto xingdao 行道 practice the Dao in each household. At night there is aprocession of lanterns.Birthday celebrations of the gods3/28: The birthday celebration of Dongyue zhusheng dadi 东岳注生大帝 isheld with opera performances.5/19: The birthday celebration of Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtufuren 后土夫人 is held with opera performances.

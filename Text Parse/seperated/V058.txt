V058 尾厝 Weicuo1. Village settlement Weicuo is part of the Gangxi Administrative Village港西行政村. The population of Weicuo village is approximately 300 people.2. Surname groups The main surname groups in Weicuo village are thePeng 彭 and the Wu 吴.3. Village temples Damafu 大妈府 is dedicated to Cuifu dama 崔府大妈.4. Rituals: Yuanxiao processions On 1/18 villagers invite Fazhu xianfei法主仙妃 to go on a procession around the village.Birthday celebrations of the gods6/20: the celebration of Cuifu dama 崔府大妈 is held with opera.

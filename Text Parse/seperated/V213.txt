V213 Chencuo 陈厝1. Village settlement Chencuo is part of the Daqiu Administrative Village大丘行政村. The current population of Chencuo village is approximately400 people.2. Surname groups The main surname groups are the Zheng 郑, theSu 苏, and the Lin 林. Each year each group selects by rotation one Fushou福首.3. Village temples Jurengong 聚仁宫 was built in Kangxi 54 (1715) anddemolished in 1958. In 1980 it was rebuilt, and is now the main overall Pugong铺宫 temple of the thirty-six village alliance of Hushi. Currently, representatives from each of the thirty-six villages still sit on the temple management

V021 Xiwei 西尾1. Village settlement Xiwei village is part of the Zhengban Administrative Village 郑阪行政村. The village has a population of approximately400 people.2. Surname groups The main surname group is the Xu 徐.3. Village temples 1) Yonganshê 永安社 is dedicated to Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王, Houtu furen 后土夫人. The secondary god is Zhushengdadi 注生大帝.4.

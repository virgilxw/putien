V081 Dingsong 顶宋1. Village settlement Dingsong is part of the Dongjiao AdministrativeVillage 东郊行政村. The entire village has approximately 600 people.2. Surname groups The Song 宋 makes up more than 95% of the population.3. Village temples 1) Hulan biegong 壶兰别宫 contains the altar of theLiantang shushê 莲塘书社. The temple is dedicated to Qitian dasheng 齐天大圣, Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. Secondarygods are Tianshang shengmu 天上圣母, Sima shengwang 司马圣王, andSanxianshi 三仙师.2) Baoshandong 宝山洞 is dedicated to Daye 大爷 and Erye 二爷.3) Baoshandian 宝山殿 is dedicated to Wuhuang dadi 五皇大帝. Secondarygods are Yutong jiangjun 玉通将军and Yuzhao xianshi 玉昭仙师.4) Xihuci 西湖祠 is dedicated to Lin Lonjiang 林龙江. The SJCDCB, 1992:16claims 420 initiates.4.

V022 Cangtou 苍头1. Village settlement Cangtou village is part of the Zhengban AdministrativeVillage 郑阪行政村. Cangtou has approximately 300 people.2. Surname groups The main surname group is the Liao 廖. There are alsoten Fu 傅 households.3. Village temples 1) Yongxing shangshê 永兴上社, Baiyungong 白云宫is dedicated to Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王, Houtu furen 后土夫人, Kongfudaren 孔府大人, Zhanggong shengjun 张公圣君, Lufu dashen 鲁府大神,and Lufu furen 鲁府夫人.4. Rituals: Yuanxiao processions On 1/15 villagers take the incenseburner of the shê altar on a procession to each household to xingdao 行道practice the Dao.Birthday celebrations of the gods8/4: Birthday celebration of Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王, Houtu furen后土夫人. Opera is performed.

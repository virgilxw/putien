V076 Dalu 大路1. Village settlement Dalu is part of the Xiaban Administrative Village 下阪行政村. Dalu village has a population of over 200 people. The great majorityof these villagers are Christians.2. Surname groups Dalu is a single surname village. The only surname isHe 何.3. Village temples Wanxing xishê 万兴西社 is dedicated to Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen 后土夫人. The secondary god is Wencaowupan 文曹武判.4. Rituals: Yuanxiao processions On 1/12 the villagers take the incenseburners of the shê altar 社炉 and palm sedan chairs 棕轿 on a processionto each household to xingdao 行道 practice the Dao and to beg the godsfor blessings and happiness 乞喜. On 1/22–23 a common Yuanxiao 总元宵is held. Tianshang shengmu 天上圣母 and Sima shengwang 司马圣王 areinvited to go on procession throughout the sevenfold ritual alliance.Birthday celebrations of the gods10/14: The celebration of Zunzhu mingwang 尊主明王 and Houtu furen后土夫人. Daoist ritual masters are invited to hold a jiao ritual做醮.
